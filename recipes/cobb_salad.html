Cobb Salad Recipe

Description

This cobb salad features some of my favorite ingredients: bacon, blue cheese, tomato, chicken, green onion, egg, and topped with ranch dressing.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 3 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • 3/4 cup blue cheese, crumbled
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle Ranch-style salad dressing

Steps

  1. Make some hardboiled eggs
    1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover completely with cold water.
    2. Bring to a boil then cover and remove from heat.
      3. Let eggs sit for 10 to 12 minutes, then cool, peel and chop.
  2. Make some bacon
    1. While the eggs are cooking, place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
    2. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown, 7 to 10 minutes.
    3. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
  3. Season, dice, and cook boneless chicken breast
  4. Place shredded lettuce into big bowl
  5. Top with rows of egg, bacon, blue cheese, tomato, chicken, green onion, and egg
  6. Drizzle with ranch dressing
