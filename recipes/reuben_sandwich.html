Reuben Sandwich Recipe

Description

A Reuben sandwich is a grilled sandwich featuring corned beef on rye bread.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of rye bread
  • 1 Ounce of thousand island dressing
  • 2 Slices of Swiss cheese
  • 2 Slices of deli sliced corned beef
  • 1/4 Cup Sauerkraut, drained
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter, softened

Steps

  1. Preheat a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Spread thousand island dressing over one side of both bread slices
  3. Add 1 slice of Swiss cheese
  4. Add 2 slices of corned beef
  5. Add 1/4 cup of Sauerkraut
  6. Add a 2nd slice of Swiss Cheese
  7. Top with remaining slice of bread with dressing-side down
  8. Butter the top of the sandwich
  9. Place sandwich butterside down in the large skillet to cook
  10. Butter the top of the sandwich while it cooks
  11. Grill about 5 minutes per side or until golden brown
  12. Serve hot!
